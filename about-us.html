About Us

What is our story?

What is Americode, and how did it come to be? It all started from a Programming bootcamp, out of Rutgers NJ. Three young men decided they didn't wanna get out of the camp and be left out to dry. Three young met by chance and they clicked right away, they decided that they didnt want to work for someone else, they wanted to creat there perfect job, but not only for them but future employees because they know how hard the job field can be on new developers, that is why we have a special program that hires interns and we craft them from the start. One day they were just shouting out names and we decided that we love America and we love doing, so we put that together and Americode was created. We opened this business to create jobs and to create a standard for quality work.

What do we do?

What does Americode do? Well we have a business plan set up in three parts, we specialize in Web Development, Web Advertising/Marketing and Web maintenance, and we also help our client on the design and can even help with there logo!.
